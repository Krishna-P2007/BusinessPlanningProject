# Possible changes
1) Headcount plan for employees:
     Current: Fixed number of employees per year
     Change: Headcount growth rate

2) Inventory:
     Current: Inventory is constant, and clumped together.
     Change: Inventory could scale with increasing sales, also divide inventory based on product because DIO may not be same for all products

3) Payables and receivables:
     Current : APs and ARs are kept as 0
     Change: In a B2B business, 30-90 day payment periods are common. So ARs should at least be included.

4) Operating costs:
     Current: Op costs are set as constant, only influenced by CPI
     Change: The would probably scale with revenue (which is growing much faster)

5) Gross margins:
     Current: small numbers like 35% for hardware
     Change: This is unrealistic, COGS will take up a higher portion of selling price

6) More costs:
     Current: We have included a good amount of costs
     Change: Could add distributor margins, freight, R&D, etc.
