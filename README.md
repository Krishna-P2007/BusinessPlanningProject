# README

## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive business and financial analysis for **Sahasraksh Deep Tech**, an AI-driven industrial inspection company developing autonomous drones, rovers, and distributed computing infrastructure for predictive asset management. The project combines strategic planning, financial modelling, and product documentation into a single integrated framework that can be used for business planning, financial analysis, and decision-making.

Rather than serving as a simple collection of documents, the project demonstrates how a technology startup can be evaluated from multiple perspectives, including its business strategy, operational model, technical architecture, financial viability, and long-term growth potential.

---

## Project Components

The project consists of three primary documents.

### 1. Financial Model (Excel)

The financial model provides a complete long-term projection of the company's operations and finances. It includes:

* Assumption-driven forecasting
* Revenue projections by region and product
* Pricing and market growth assumptions
* Cost modelling
* Income Statement
* Balance Sheet
* Cash Flow Statement
* Working capital calculations
* Inventory planning using an EOQ-based approach
* Bill of Materials (BOM)
* Loan amortisation schedule
* Depreciation and amortisation schedules
* Free Cash Flow to Equity (FCFE) valuation
* Investment metrics and financial analysis

The model has been designed so that changing assumptions automatically updates the entire financial forecast, making it suitable for scenario analysis and business planning.

---

### 2. Business Plan

The business plan describes the strategic direction of the company and explains:

* Company vision and mission
* Problem statement
* Proposed solution
* Product roadmap
* Technology architecture
* Market opportunity
* Target customers
* Business model
* Revenue strategy
* Marketing and sales strategy
* Operations
* Management structure
* Funding requirements
* Growth roadmap

It serves as the strategic foundation upon which the financial model has been constructed.

---

### 3. Company Brochure

The brochure provides a concise overview of the company's offerings, including:

* Company introduction
* Four-tier architecture
* Product portfolio
* AI capabilities
* Core technologies
* Industrial applications
* Customer use cases
* Benefits of the platform
* Performance metrics

Its purpose is to communicate the company's value proposition in a clear and visually accessible format.

## Objectives of the Project

The primary objective of this project is to develop a coherent and internally consistent representation of a deep-tech startup operating in the industrial AI and autonomous inspection space.

Specifically, the project aims to:

* Translate a business concept into a financially viable operating model.
* Demonstrate how strategic assumptions influence financial performance.
* Evaluate the commercial feasibility of an AI-enabled industrial inspection platform.
* Connect technical architecture with business strategy and financial outcomes.
* Provide a structured framework for analysing operational scalability, profitability, and capital requirements.
* Illustrate the interaction between product development, market expansion, and long-term financial performance.

---

## Financial Model Design Philosophy

The financial model is designed around an assumptions-driven approach. Rather than hardcoding values, nearly every calculation is linked to a central assumptions sheet, allowing users to explore different business scenarios with minimal changes.

Key modelling features include:

* Regional revenue forecasting
* CPI-linked pricing escalation
* Market-driven sales growth
* Subscription churn modelling
* Hardware and software revenue separation
* Dynamic cost assumptions
* EOQ-based inventory management
* Working capital forecasting
* Integrated financial statements
* Automated depreciation and amortisation
* Loan repayment modelling
* FCFE-based valuation

This modular structure improves transparency, maintainability, and flexibility while reducing modelling errors.
