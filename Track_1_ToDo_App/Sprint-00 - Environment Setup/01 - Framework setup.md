# Build a framework of a business plan

<summary>What is a framework??</summary>

It is a skeleton of a business plan, which contains the necessary information that will be used to actually make one.

It need not conatin any numbers , just a basic idea of what your company is, and a brief analysis of paths forward

It will be put down in a presentation format, containing basic information such as possible pricing models, a small list of competitors and what we can learn from them, etc.
